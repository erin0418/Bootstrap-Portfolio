About Me

My puppy is a 2 year old Siberian Husky named Nymeria and my cat is a Calico cat named Khaleesi who we have had for 6 years now.

I have had a long travel to get to where I am today. After graduating high school, I spent a year at Penn State pursuing a degree in science. From there I jumped into the buisness end of jobs. I worked as an office assisstant at a doctors office, a shift mananger at Pizza Hut, and now today as an office manager.

Right now as office manager I work at an after school tutoring center called Math Genie. There I am the manager of the East Brunswick location running all daily operations. While there I started working on developing the company's website into the site it is today. This included redesigning not only the athsetics that the customers see, but also what the search engine sees. In doing this, I once again became interested in coding as a profession as well as a passion.

Completing this coding bootcamp while working full time will definitely be a challenge. Overall, I am a very ambitious person, so will make sure to balance my schedule accordingly. Whenever I sit down to code I see it more as enjoyable and fun than a chore. I hope you enjoy the rest of the site and my portfolio items!
